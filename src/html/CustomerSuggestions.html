Customer Suggestions

If you have any idea on how to improve GoodNotes, feel free to share it with us and other users. While we can't respond to every suggestion, we are committed to reading every post.

How it works -

  • Check out the other ideas that have been suggested and vote on your favorites
  • If your idea isn't listed, submit your own - use a descriptive title with 20 words or less, please. Include the details in the description field
  • Include only one suggestion per post
  • Help us and others understand you better and explain why and when you need the feature

If something is not working as expected or you need help, please visit our help center instead. We can't offer customer support in the suggestion forum.
